07 — FAQ

Everything else
you might ask.

How do I confirm a booking?

Pick a tour or car from this page, fill in your date/time/pax in the booking sheet, and hit "Book on WhatsApp." Your booking summary auto-fills the chat. We reply within 30 minutes during business hours (8am–10pm Malaysia time). A 30% deposit secures the slot.

What payment methods do you accept?

For deposit: online bank transfer (Maybank, CIMB, Public Bank), Touch 'n Go eWallet, or Wise. For balance on the day: cash (MYR), bank transfer, or eWallet. We do not currently accept credit cards or USD/SGD cash.

What is your cancellation policy?

Tours and rentals can be cancelled free of charge up to 48 hours before the start time — full deposit refund. Within 48 hours, we hold the deposit but allow rescheduling within 30 days at no cost. No-shows forfeit the deposit. We never cancel on you unless there's a weather hazard, in which case we reschedule or refund 100%.

What happens if it rains?

Light rain: tours run as normal — Langkawi rain is usually short. Heavy rain or storm warning: we proactively reach out the night before to either reschedule, switch to indoor stops (Sky Bridge, Underwater World, museums), or refund. Mangrove kayak and JetSki are weather-dependent and cannot run in stormy conditions.

Are children allowed on tours?

Yes, tours and transport are family-friendly. Free child seats (booster or full car seat) on request — please let us know the child's age when booking. ATV is age 12+. JetSki passengers must be 6+. Mangrove kayak and sunset cruise welcome all ages with provided life jackets.

Do you offer halal food options on tours?

Yes. All restaurant stops we recommend on tours are halal-certified or Muslim-friendly by default. Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options are easy to accommodate at most stops — just mention it when booking and we'll route accordingly.

Where do you pick up from?

Free hotel lobby pickup across Pantai Cenang, Pantai Tengah, Kuah, and Padang Matsirat for tours of 4 hours and above. We also pick up from Langkawi International Airport (LGK) and Kuah Jetty. Pickup outside these zones: small RM 20–40 fuel surcharge depending on distance.

How does rental fuel work?

Full-to-full. We deliver the car with a full tank — please return it full. If you return with less than full, we refuel at pump price + RM 20 service fee. The RM 300 refundable deposit covers any fuel deficit, damages, or traffic summonses found during inspection.

Do you provide GPS or Waze setup?

All cars come with a phone holder & USB charger so you can run Waze or Google Maps on your own phone. We can pre-load a curated map of recommended food, beach, and viewpoint stops on request — sent as a Google Maps shared list before your pickup.

What if I need to extend my rental?

Just WhatsApp us before the original return time — extensions are easy and charged at the same daily rate. Extra hours (under a full day): RM 10/hour, capped at the daily rate after 8 hours.

Is the JetSki tour safe for non-swimmers?

Yes. Coast Guard-certified life jackets are mandatory for everyone, swimmers or not. Briefing is given before departure. The route stays within sheltered island channels — no open-sea crossings. A guide jet ski accompanies the group at all times.

Can I customise a tour beyond what's listed?

Absolutely — most of our regulars are repeat custom bookings. Mix transport with hidden waterfalls, late-night durian runs, photography spots at sunrise, or a private temple visit. WhatsApp us your interests and we'll quote a tailored price within an hour.
