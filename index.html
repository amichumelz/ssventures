SS Langkawi Ventures provides complete travel & tour services in Langkawi — airport transfers, half-day & full-day sightseeing tours with experienced drivers. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the trip.

Experienced Drivers

All our drivers are licensed, courteous, and know Langkawi inside out — from hidden food stalls and beaches to scenic mountain spots, waterfalls, and tourist attractions across the island. They speak Bahasa Malaysia, English, and basic Mandarin.

All-Inclusive Pricing

Driver, fuel, toll, and parking — all included in the package price. Pay easily via DuitNow, FPX, credit/debit card, or cash. No surprises, no hidden charges.

24/7 Hotline

Last-minute booking? Flight delayed? Need to extend your tour? Just call or WhatsApp +60 11‑2661 0913. We're always ready to help.

    Good to Know

    Before you book

    Book in Advance

    Recommended at least 24 hours ahead, especially during weekends & school holidays. Same-day bookings subject to availability.

    Pickup Anywhere

    Hotel lobby, airport arrival hall, ferry terminal, or your home. Just share the location pin via WhatsApp — easy.

    Flexible Timing

    Half-Day = 1-4 hours, Full-Day = 5-8 hours. Need extra time? RM 50-200 per additional hour depending on vehicle. No rigid schedules.

    Easy Payment

    Pay 50% deposit on confirmation, 50% balance on the day. We accept DuitNow, FPX, credit/debit card, or cash.
